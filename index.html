Filing of Candidacy

March 1–11

The leaders we need are not the ones who feel the most confident, but the ones who care enough about their community to try.

Download the Form →

All aspiring candidates must prepare and submit the following documentary requirements:

  • Duly accomplished Certificate of Candidacy (COC) with a 2×2 ID photo
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Grade Slip from the previous semester
  • Certificate of Registration (COR) for the current semester
  • Endorsement Letters from the Dean and Department Head. If no Department Head, the Dean's signature suffices
  • Updated Curriculum Vitae
  • A comprehensive General Plan of Action, whether independent or under a political party
  • Political parties must submit as a group — incomplete party = automatically independent
  • All documents in a clear plastic envelope, arranged by position for parties

Refer to the SSC Constitution and By-Laws (CBL) for candidate qualifications.

Send an email to the Political Electoral Committee with your intended submission date and time — for documentation and to formally signal your intention to file.

All candidates must wear white attire as a symbol of transparency and neutrality. Same-party candidates must appear together when submitting.

Contact: ssc.elecom2026@gmail.com

Proceed to the Office of Student Affairs (OSA) Hall and formally submit your COC and complete requirements to the:

  • Head of Transparency and Accountability
  • Chief Commissioner
