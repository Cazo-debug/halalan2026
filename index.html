All aspiring candidates must prepare and submit the following documentary requirements:

  • Duly accomplished Certificate of Candidacy (COC) with a 2Ã—2 ID photo
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Grade Slip from the previous semester
  • Certificate of Registration (COR) for the current semester
  • Endorsement Letters from the Dean and Department Head. In cases where the College has no Department Head, the endorsement shall bear the signature of the respective Dean
  • Updated Curriculum Vitae
  • A comprehensive General Plan of Action, whether running independently or under a political party
  • Political parties must submit requirements as a group. Failure to include members shall automatically classify the candidate/s as independent
  • All documents must be compiled in a clear plastic envelope. For party submissions, documents must be properly arranged according to position

Applicants are advised to refer to the SSC Constitution and By-Laws (CBL) for further information regarding candidate qualifications.

Candidates must send an email to the Political Electoral Committee indicating the date and time they intend to submit their requirements â€” for documentation purposes and to formally signify their intention to file for candidacy.

All candidates are required to wear white attire as a symbol of transparency, neutrality, and commitment to a fair electoral process.

All candidates must be present during submission. Candidates belonging to the same political party must appear together when submitting their documents.

Candidates shall proceed to the Office of Student Affairs (OSA) Hall and formally submit their Certificates of Candidacy and complete requirements to the:

  • Head of Transparency and Accountability
  • Chief Commissioner
